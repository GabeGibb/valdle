Last Updated: 4/13/2024

Welcome to valdle.gg. This privacy policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information we receive through our website. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information Collection

We do not directly collect personal information from our visitors. However, we use Google Analytics, which collects data to provide insights into website usage.

2. Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a web analytics service offered by Google that tracks and reports website traffic. This service may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our website that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data.

3. Use of Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. They are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device.

4. Data Usage

The data collected by Google Analytics is used to analyze the use of our website and to improve user experience. We do not have access to personally identifiable information in this process. For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to opt out of data collection by Google Analytics. You can adjust your cookie settings in your browser.

6. Data Protection

We take data protection seriously and ensure that any data collected is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
