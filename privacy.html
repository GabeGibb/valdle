Privacy Policy for valdle.gg

Last Updated: 10/23/2024

Welcome to valdle.gg. This privacy policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information we receive through our website. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information Collection

We do not directly collect personal information from our visitors. However, we use Google Analytics and ExoClick, which collect data to provide insights into website usage and serve relevant ads.

2. Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a web analytics service offered by Google that tracks and reports website traffic. This service may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, browser version, the pages you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data.

3. ExoClick

We use ExoClick to serve ads and provide personalization services on this website. ExoClick collects certain data to deliver relevant ads based on user behavior and preferences. ExoClick’s privacy policy can be reviewed here. For additional privacy-related inquiries or concerns, you can visit ExoClick’s privacy center.

4. Use of Cookies

Google Analytics and ExoClick use cookies to collect information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. They are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. Cookies help improve user experience and deliver relevant ads.

5. Data Usage

The data collected by Google Analytics and ExoClick is used to analyze the use of our website, improve user experience, and serve targeted ads. We do not have access to personally identifiable information in this process.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to opt out of data collection by Google Analytics and ExoClick. You can adjust your cookie settings in your browser.

7. Data Protection

We take data protection seriously and ensure that any data collected is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
